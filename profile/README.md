## What we do

Tequity is a **secure data room** platform. We give teams a controlled space to store, share, and track confidential documents — the kind of material that moves through due diligence, fundraising, M&A, and board and investor reporting.

Every document stays under your control: fine-grained permissions decide who sees what, and a complete audit trail records every view, download, and change.

## Focus areas

| | |
|---|---|
| 🔐 **Security & access control** | Granular, per-document and per-user permissions so the right people see exactly what they should — and nothing more. |
| 📁 **Data rooms** | Organised, searchable spaces for confidential documents, built for due diligence and deal flow. |
| 🧾 **Audit & activity** | A complete, exportable record of every view, download, and change — for accountability and compliance. |
| 🚀 **Modern workspace** | A fast, focused interface that makes sharing sensitive material feel effortless. |
| 🔗 **Built on verJSON** | Powered by the verJSON platform toolchain for reliable, well-tested delivery. |
